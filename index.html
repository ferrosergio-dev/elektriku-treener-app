About the App

Elektriku Treener is a free, ad-free, fully offline exam prep app created by an electrician for electricians in Estonia. It helps you confidently prepare for level 3, 4, and 5 qualification exams with realistic simulations, instant feedback, and personalized progress tracking — all without internet.

Key Features
1064 Questions
Across all levels and topics (Safety, Measurement, Installation, etc.)
Smart Practice Mode
Shuffled questions + randomized answers — learn concepts, not memorize layouts
Real Exam Simulation
50–80 questions; 70% pass mark with instant feedback
Progress Tracking
Three independent "knowledge batteries" (Levels 3/4/5) fill as you learn
Trilingual Support
English, Estonian, and Russian with auto-detection
Complete Privacy
All data stays on your device — fully GDPR compliant
